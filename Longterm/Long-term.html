Long-Term

  • Confusion - Users under the influence might feel random occurances of confusion, another effect that starts as short-term but over time progresses into a long-term.
  • Addiction - Meth is highly addictive and can increase chances or getting addicted to other drugs.
  • Insomnia - At first insomnia is a short-term effect, but after chronic use of Methamphetamine the effects of insomnia can become long-term.
