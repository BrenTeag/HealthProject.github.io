Short-term Effects

  • Confusion - Users under the influence might feel random occurances of confusion.
  • Mood Disturbances - Users under the influence might have constant mood swings.
  • Aggression - While under the influence of Methamphetamine the user might feel short tempered and easily irratated.
  • Insomnia - While under the influence of Methamphetamine the user may experience difficulties falling asleep these effects can also turn into a long-term situation.
  • While under the influence of Methamphetamine the user might experience a sensation as if bugs were crawling under your skin, this can make the user scratch at their skin until they are bleeding.
